Dough Making Instructions

  1. Cool water to below 60°F.
  2. Mix the water and yeast until fully dissolved.
  3. Add flour and Olive Oil and mix for 2 minutes.
  4. Keep mixing on low speed, then add Sugar and Salt.
  5. Mix for 10 more minutes until dough is smooth and elastic.
  6. Cover the dough with plastic wrap and rest for 1–3 hours.
  7. Divide the dough into equal portions.
  8. Shape into balls, sealing the seam tightly on the bottom.
  9. Place dough balls in lightly oiled containers.
  10. Cover tightly and refrigerate for 2–4 days. 3 days is ideal.
  11. Let the dough come to room temperature before using (1–2 hours).
